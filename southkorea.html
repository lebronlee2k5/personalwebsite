South Korea Must Go Destinations

Seoul

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Stepping into Gyeongbokgung Palace felt like traveling back in time. The intricate architecture and serene courtyards offered a peaceful escape from the city's hustle. Seeing people in Hanbok added to the magical atmosphere.

How to get there:
Easily accessible by Seoul's subway system (Gyeongbokgung Station, Line 3)

N Seoul Tower

The views from N Seoul Tower were breathtaking, watching the city lights twinkle at night was incredibly romantic.

How to get there:
Via cable car from the base of Namsan Mountain, which is accessible by bus or subway.

Bukchon Hanok Village

Wandering through Bukchon Hanok Village was like discovering a hidden gem. The traditional houses and quiet alleyways offered a glimpse into old Seoul.

How to get there:
Accessible by Seoul's subway system (Anguk Station, Line 3)

Jeju Island

Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak)

Hiking Seongsan Ilchulbong at sunrise will probably be a unforgettable experience. The panoramic views of the ocean and surrounding landscape were simply stunning.

How to get there:
Fly from Seoul's Gimpo Airport to Jeju International Airport, then take local buses or taxis.

Jeongbang Waterfall

Seeing a waterfall cascade directly into the ocean was incredible, Jeongbang Waterfall is a unique and beautiful natural wonder.

How to get there:
Fly from Seoul's Gimpo Airport to Jeju International Airport, then take local buses or taxis.

Manjanggul Lava Tube

Exploring a lava tube, that sounds unreal, Manjanggul Lava Tube looks like a must-see. I'm so interested in seeing those unique rock formations.

How to get there:
Accessible by bus from Jeju City.
