Singapore Must Go Destinations

Marina Bay

Gardens by the Bay

Famous for its Supertree Grove, Cloud Forest, and Flower Dome, this futuristic park blends nature and technology with stunning landscapes and nightly light shows.

How to get there:
Take the MRT to Bayfront Station (CE1/DT16), Exit B, and walk via the Dragonfly Bridge.

Marina Bay Sands SkyPark

The SkyPark offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Singapore skyline and is an architectural marvel.

How to get there:
Take the MRT to Bayfront Station (CE1/DT16), and follow signs to Marina Bay Sands.

Sentosa Island

Universal Studios Singapore

A world-class theme park with thrilling rides and attractions based on blockbuster movies.

How to get there:
Take the MRT to HarbourFront Station (NE1/CC29), then take the Sentosa Express monorail to Waterfront Station.

S.E.A. Aquarium

One of the world's largest aquariums, home to 100,000 marine animals across 50 habitats.

How to get there:
Take the Sentosa Express monorail to Waterfront Station from HarbourFront MRT (NE1/CC29).

Adventure Cove Waterpark

A thrilling water park featuring high-speed slides, wave pools, and snorkeling with tropical fish.

How to get there:
Take the Sentosa Express monorail to Waterfront Station from HarbourFront MRT.
