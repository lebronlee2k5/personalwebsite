Japan Must Go Destinations

Tokyo

Akihabara

As an Anime Fan, I want to experience the Anime Culture in Japan.

How to get there:
Take the JR Tokaido Shinkansen bullet train from Tokyo Station to Utsunomiya Station, then transfer to the JR Tohoku Line to Akihabara Station.

Tokyo Tower

I want to see the Tokyo Tower lights up beautifully at night.

How to get there:
Several bus companies operate services from various points in Tokyo to the Tokyo Skytree area.

Tokyo Disneyland

I want to try Disneyland theme park atleast once in my life.

How to get there:
Take the JR Nambu Line from Tokyo Station to Maihara Station, then transfer to the Keio Line to Shinjuku Station. From there, take the Odakyu Line to Karakawa Station, and finally take the Resort Liner to Tokyo Disney Resort.

Kyoto Prefecture

Fushimi Inari Taisha

I want to experience this most visited shrine in kyoto, since they said it was magical.

How to get there:
Take the JR Nambu Line to Kurume Station.
Transfer to the JR Kyushu Shinkansen to Hakata Station.
Take the JR Saga Line to Saga Station.
Transfer to the JR Nara Line to Fushimi Inari Station.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

I want to visit Arashiyama Bamboo Grove for its serene beauty, the unique experience of walking through towering bamboo stalks, and the scenery that offers a escape from the city.

How to get there:
Take the Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line from Tokyo Station to Kyoto Station, then transfer to the Keihan Main Line to Arashiyama Station.
