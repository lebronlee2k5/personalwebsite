HongKong Must Go Destinations

Hong Kong Island

Victoria Peak

To witness the breathtaking, panoramic spectacle of Hong Kong's iconic skyline and Victoria Harbour. The views are especially magical at sunset and night, when the city transforms into a sea of twinkling lights.

How to get there:
From Central MTR Station, take Exit J2, and follow the signs for a short walk to the Peak Tram Lower Terminus.

Man Mo Temple

A historic Taoist temple with unique incense coils and a peaceful atmosphere, providing a tranquil sanctuary amidst the city's hustle and bustle.

How to get there:
MTR to Central or Sheung Wan Stations, Mid-Levels Escalator.

Ocean Park Hong kong

A blend of theme park thrills and marine animal encounters, with stunning coastal views, offering a diverse range of attractions.

How to get there:
MTR South Island Line to Ocean Park Station.

Kowloon

Temple Street Night Market (Yau Ma Tei)

A lively street market with local food, souvenirs, and fortune-telling stalls, offering a taste of local Hong Kong life and street culture.

How to get there:
MTR to Jordan or Yau Ma Tei Stations.

Tsim Sha Tsui Pier

An iconic and affordable harbour crossing, offering stunning, unobstructed views of the city's architectural wonders and bustling harbour activity.

How to get there:
MTR to Tsim Sha Tsui.
